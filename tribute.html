Billie Eilish

About Billie

Billie Eilish is an American singer and songwriter. She was born on 18th December 2001 in Los Angeles, California.

Key events

  1. 2015 – “Ocean Eyes” Release
  2. 2019 – Debut Album & Grammy Sweep -youngest artist ever to win the “Big Four” Grammys (Album, Record, Song of the Year, and Best New Artist) in one night.
  3. 2020 – James Bond Theme “No Time to Die”- youngest artist to write and record a James Bond theme, which later won an Oscar, Golden Globe, and Grammy.
  4. 2021 – Second Album & Met Gala Icon

Key inventions

  • Whisper-Pop Vocal Style - Popularized a soft, intimate, almost ASMR-like singing technique.
  • Genre-Bending Aesthetic - signature sound that inspired a wave of minimalist, moody pop.
  • Distinct Visual Identity- Helped “invent” a recognizable oversized-streetwear-meets-high-fashion look—neon hair, baggy clothes—to challenge beauty standards and promote body autonomy.
